✦ The Origin

A GARAGE.
A BROKEN GPU.
A BREAKTHROUGH.

In 2021, two engineering graduates — working out of a rented garage with a thermal camera, salvaged circuit boards, and zero funding — discovered something the entire semiconductor industry had missed for decades.

While trying to fix a failing GPU, they stumbled onto a method of routing neural computations through photonic circuits — using light instead of electricity. The result was processing speeds that defied everything on the market.

They didn't plan to build a company. They planned to publish a paper. But when they realized what they had, the paper became a prototype. The prototype became SOLUM CORE. And SOLUM CORE changed everything.
